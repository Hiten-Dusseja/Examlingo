× Give Exam Marking Scheme and Instruction Result

Navigation:
You can navigate through out the website using the side bar menu on the left side of every page.

Time:
You will get a total of 50 mins, to complete the exam.
The timer is there on the left side of the questions in the 'Give Exam tab'

Question:
You will get a total of 40 questions, to complete the exam. Out of which every question weighs for 1 mark
The question grid is there on the right side of the questions in the 'Give Exam tab' for your reference and ease through out the exam

Result:
The result is there in the 'Result tab'. The result won't change again until you resubmit the exam

Printing the Result:
You can print the result by clicking on the 'Print Result' button in the 'Result tab'
